# Discovery V9.5 — Bulk-Import Deep-Extraction

## Idea / main goal

Raise the knowledge quality the bulk-email importer feeds into the company handbook (Exit-Readiness asset). Today the bulk path is a **deliberately flat single-pass** pipeline; the proven **Analyst↔Challenger multi-pass principle** lives only in the condensation (questionnaire) pipeline. Lift the *principle* (not the code) onto bulk patterns.

## Code-grounded findings (not from memory)

- **Flat bulk extractor:** `src/workers/bulk-email/handle-pattern-extraction-job.ts` — per redacted `email_thread`, exactly **one** Sonnet call (`extractPatternFromThread`) → 1..5 `email_pattern` rows. No refine loop, no critic, **no cross-thread synthesis** (each thread isolated; no dedup/merge/frequency-weighting). Cost-cap guarded (`checkLiveCapInWorker`, `V9_BULK_EMAIL_RUN_CAP_EUR`). SLC-167 went flat *by design* for cost.
- **Multi-pass condensation:** `src/workers/condensation/iteration-loop.ts` — Analyst→Challenger, 2–8 iterations until `ACCEPTED`/`ACCEPTED_WITH_NOTES`, orchestrator gating. Operates on questionnaire `BlockDefinition`s.
- **Model drift (ISSUE-100):** bulk extractor defaults to **Sonnet 3.5** (`anthropic.claude-3-5-sonnet-20241022-v2:0`, `src/lib/ai/bedrock-sonnet/email-pattern.ts:51`) while the condensation core runs **eu-Sonnet-4**.

## The "lift multi-pass" idea is three features under one name

| Lever | Value | Cost reality | V9.5? |
|---|---|---|---|
| A. Cross-thread synthesis (dedup/merge/frequency-weight raw patterns → consolidated units) | Highest | Cheap — runs on compact extracted patterns, not raw threads | **IN** |
| B. Per-thread Analyst↔Challenger loop | Medium | Expensive — 2–8× per-thread cost, fights SLC-167 cost-cap | **Parked** |
| C. Corpus gap/theme-completeness detection | Strategic | Large separate feature | **Parked** |

Key insight: the naive reading ("Challenger per thread") is the worst cost-for-value option. The real win is the critic/refine principle at the **synthesis layer**, where data is already small and consolidation is where quality lives.

## Founder decisions (locked this session)

1. **Depth target → Cross-thread synthesis + critic gate** (keep cheap per-thread pass).
2. **Cost appetite → Bounded, predictable** (fixed 1–2 synthesis/critic passes, hard cost-cap preserved; no convergence runaway).
3. **Model cleanup → Include in V9.5** (move bulk to eu-Sonnet-4 + fix the 4 stale model-default files, ISSUE-100).

## V9.5 scope

**IN:** new cross-thread synthesis stage after pattern-extraction (dedup/merge/consolidate `email_pattern` → higher-quality candidate units, frequency/evidence weighting) · a bounded Challenger/quality-gate pass on the synthesized output · bulk-path eu-Sonnet-4 model cleanup (ISSUE-100).

**OUT:** per-thread multi-pass loop · corpus gap/theme-completeness detection · curation-UI changes beyond what synthesis feeds · cross-source (emails + questionnaire) synthesis.
