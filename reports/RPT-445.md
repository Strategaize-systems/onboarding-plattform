#### Low

This is the **correct pragmatic behavior** for the current phase: with IONOS sync not yet live (OQ-R1-1), `received` is always false, so gating would block activation entirely. Re-evaluate when IMAP sync is live. Report-only, not filed.

Mitigated by: the new `suggestSetup` hermetic tests, static wiring verification, and `next build` compile-PASS. Browser/interaction verification is the IONOS-gated Live-Smoke.
